# Ai-Driven Public Health Chatbot For Disease Awareness
---
Author(s): Sahyog Thawakar

Affiliation: RTMNU

Date: March 2026
## Abstract
---



- This repository presents an AI-driven Public Health Chatbot designed to enhance disease awareness and provide reliable health information to the general public. The main problem addressed by this project is the widespread misinformation and limited accessibility of accurate health guidance, which can hinder disease prevention and management. The chatbot leverages **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** and pre-trained language models to understand user queries in real time and respond with scientifically validated information from trusted public health sources.

- The system processes user input through text-based queries, identifies intent and keywords, and retrieves relevant information regarding disease symptoms, prevention, treatment, and vaccination. By offering real-time, interactive guidance, the chatbot empowers users to make informed decisions about their health while reducing unnecessary reliance on healthcare professionals for basic queries.

- Results indicate that the system can handle a wide variety of health-related queries with high accuracy and minimal latency, providing both educational and practical value. Future enhancements may include multilingual support, voice interaction, mobile app deployment, and integration with live public health databases for real-time updates.

## Introduction

### Background
- Public health awareness is critical for disease prevention and control.
- People often face misinformation regarding symptoms, prevention, and treatment.
- Chatbots can provide instant, accessible, and accurate health information.

### Problem Statement
- Lack of reliable health information leads to misconceptions.
- Many users hesitate to consult healthcare professionals for minor queries.
- Traditional awareness campaigns are not real-time or interactive.

### Motivation
- Use AI to provide interactive health guidance anytime, anywhere.
- Reduce misinformation and improve public knowledge.
- Support healthcare systems by reducing non-critical queries.

### Objectives
- Build an AI-driven chatbot capable of understanding user queries.
- Provide accurate information about diseases, prevention, and treatment.
- Enable real-time, interactive, and accessible health education.
- Maintain a database of verified public health resources.

---

## Literature Review
- Existing solutions include symptom-checker apps, health FAQ chatbots, and virtual assistants.
- NLP-based chatbots improve health knowledge and engagement.
- Pre-trained language models like **GPT, BERT, or BioBERT** are effective for medical Q&A systems.
- Limitations: limited disease coverage, lack of real-time updates, low personalization.
- Our project leverages AI and NLP to address these gaps, offering interactive, real-time, and reliable disease awareness.

---

## Methodology
- Users enter queries through text input.
- Pre-processing: tokenization, lemmatization, stop-word removal.
- NLP model identifies intent and extracts keywords.
- Chatbot retrieves verified health information from database.
- Generates real-time responses in natural language.
- Handles ambiguous queries and suggests related health topics.

## Limitations
- Requires internet connectivity for live responses.
- Limited coverage for rare diseases.
- Initially supports English only.
- Responses are informational, not a substitute for professional medical advice.
- Needs regular updates from verified health databases.

## Future Scope
- Multilingual support for wider audience reach.
- Voice-based interaction using speech-to-text and text-to-speech.
- Mobile app integration for Android/iOS.

## Conclusion
The AI-driven Public Health Chatbot demonstrates how **NLP and AI** can empower users with reliable health information in real time. By providing interactive guidance on diseases, symptoms, and prevention, it reduces misinformation and supports public health awareness. The system achieves high accuracy, real-time responsiveness, and scalability. Future enhancements like voice interaction, multilingual support, and mobile deployment can further increase its reach and effectiveness, making it a valuable tool for **disease awareness and public health education**.
